Mexican-style Dip

Description

This 4-layer Mexican-style dip can be made in just a few minutes and is usually eaten just as quickly. Serve with corn tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce can refried beans
  • 8 ounce container sour cream
  • 8 ounce jar medium salsa
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar-monterey jack cheese blend

Steps

  1. Spread the refried beans on the bottom of a casserole dish. Layer the sour cream, salsa and shredded cheese into the casserole dish. Serve with corn chips.
  2. Enjoy!
