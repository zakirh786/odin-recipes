Blueberry Jam

Description

Simple and easy, the delicious flavor of the blueberries takes center stage.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 pinch of ground cinamon

Steps

  1. Mix blueberries, sugar, lemon juice, and cinnamon in a saucepan; cook, stirring constantly, over medium heat until thickened, about 30 minutes.
  2. Enjoy!
